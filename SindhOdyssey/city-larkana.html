Larkana Sindh, Pakistan


Discover to Larkana, a city in the heart of Sindh, Pakistan, known for its rich historical heritage and cultural significance. Larkana is renowned as the birthplace of the ancient Indus Valley Civilization, with archaeological sites such as Mohenjo-daro nearby, offering a glimpse into one of the world's earliest urban centers. Explore its bustling bazaars, traditional handicrafts, and serene landscapes along the mighty Indus River. Larkana invites you to delve into its history, experience its hospitality, and discover the timeless allure of this historic city.Larkana is situated approximately 30 kilometers south of Mohenjo-daro, accessible by road. The journey from Sukkur to Larkana takes about 2 to 3 hours by road via National Highway N-65, passing through scenic rural Sindh.



Places to visit in Larkana


Bhutto Family Mausoleum


The Bhutto family mausoleum is located in Garhi Khuda Bakhsh, Larkana District, Sindh, Pakistan. This revered site serves as the final resting place for several prominent members of the Bhutto family, including Zulfikar Ali Bhutto, the founder of the Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP), and his children Murtaza Bhutto and Shahnawaz Bhutto, as well as his wife Nusrat Bhutto and daughter Benazir Bhutto, Pakistanâ€™s first female Prime Minister. The mausoleum is a significant historical and political landmark, reflecting the family's profound impact on Pakistan's modern history. It attracts numerous visitors, including political followers and tourists.


Mohenjo daro


Mohenjodaro, located in the Sindh province of Pakistan, is one of the most prominent archaeological sites of the Indus Valley Civilization, dating back to around 2500 BCE. This ancient city was one of the world's earliest urban centers, showcasing the advanced planning and sophistication of its inhabitants. The site covers approximately 300 hectares and reveals a well-organized urban layout with meticulously planned streets and impressive architectural features.


Shahnawaz Bhutto Public Library Larkana

The Shahnawaz Bhutto Public Library in Larkana stands as a beacon of knowledge and learning in the region. Named in honor of a prominent figure, the library offers a rich collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources catering to diverse interests and age groups. Its modern facilities include reading rooms, computer stations, and dedicated spaces for community events and educational programs. With a mission to promote literacy and intellectual growth, the library serves as a vital resource for students, researchers, and avid readers. It fosters a love for reading and provides a welcoming environment for lifelong learning and cultural enrichment.
